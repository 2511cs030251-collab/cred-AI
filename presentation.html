🚀 Key Features

Platform Capabilities

🧠

Alternative Credit Scoring Engine

5-factor AI model generates a 0–1000 score with sub-scores for Transaction Volume, Cash Flow Stability, Revenue Performance, Financial Discipline, and Digital Footprint.

📡

Financial Inclusion Index (FII)

7-axis radar chart covering Income Stability, Cash Flow Health, Savings Behaviour, Business Growth, Payment Discipline, Fraud Safety, and Digital Activity.

📊

Expense Behavior Analysis

Classifies monthly spend into Rent, Suppliers, Fuel, Utilities, Food, Savings, and Other. Flags discretionary spending anomalies automatically.

💡

Personalized Loan Recommendation

Repayment Capacity = Inflow − Expenses − EMI. Outputs eligible loan amount, interest rate, EMI, tenure, and AI confidence percentage.

⚡

Stress Testing & Explainable AI

Underwriters apply economic shock sliders. SHAP-inspired bar charts and natural language risk commentary explain every score change in real-time.

🔍

Fraud & Anomaly Detection

Real-time Fraud Risk badge with Isolation Forest and circular transaction detection to identify statement manipulation or credit cycling attempts.

🔒 Security & Compliance

Fully Compliant with India’s Privacy Regulatory Framework

RBI Account Aggregator (AA)

End-to-end alignment with India’s RBI-approved Account Aggregator consent framework. Borrowers grant explicit, granular data-sharing permissions before any analysis begins.

📜

Why CredAI Matters for India

India has one of the world’s largest populations of credit-invisible adults — people with stable incomes and disciplined spending, but no formal credit trail to prove it. Traditional banks cannot serve them profitably using legacy bureau scores.

  • Enables banks and NBFCs to underwrite thin-file borrowers with confidence
  • Reduces reliance on informal moneylenders charging 36%+ interest
  • Supports India’s push for financial inclusion under PM Jan Dhan Yojana

  • Empowers gig economy workers in India’s $1T informal sector
